ArtAuct Hi-Fi

High-Fidelity Art Auction App – UI/UX Portfolio Project

Project Overview

ArtAuct Hi-Fi is a high-fidelity prototype designed to transform the art auction experience by providing a seamless, user-friendly platform for art enthusiasts and collectors. The app bridges the gap between traditional art auctions and modern digital accessibility.

Problem Statement

Traditional art auctions often present challenges such as limited accessibility, complex registration processes, and a lack of real-time engagement. ArtAuct Hi-Fi addresses these issues by offering:

  • Virtual Auction Rooms for remote participation.
  • Real-Time Bidding with instant updates.
  • User-Friendly Interface for effortless navigation.

Target Users

  • Art Collectors: Individuals seeking to expand their collections with ease.
  • Artists: Creators looking to showcase and auction their work.
  • Art Enthusiasts: Those interested in exploring and engaging with art auctions.

Tools & Technologies

  • Design & Prototyping: Figma
  • User Testing: UsabilityHub
  • Collaboration: Slack

Key Features

  • Interactive Auction Interface allowing users to place bids in real-time.
  • Artist Profiles showcasing detailed information about artists and their works.
  • Secure Payment Gateway ensuring safe and seamless transactions.
  • Push Notifications to keep users informed about auction statuses and updates.

Design Process

  1. User Research: Conducted interviews with art collectors and enthusiasts to understand their pain points and expectations.
  2. Wireframing: Developed low-fidelity wireframes to outline the basic structure and flow.
  3. High-Fidelity Design: Created detailed screens in Figma, incorporating brand elements and user feedback.
  4. Prototyping: Built an interactive prototype to simulate user interactions and gather insights.
  5. Usability Testing: Performed testing sessions to identify usability issues and refine the design.

Visual Showcase

Example of the app's homepage and auction interface.

Challenges & Solutions

  • Challenge: Users miss live auctions because of time zone differences.
    Solution: Display auction times in user’s local time zone.
  • Challenge: Designing an intuitive user interface for diverse user groups.
    Solution: Conducted multiple user testing sessions to iterate and improve usability.

Outcome & Impact

The ArtAuct Hi-Fi prototype has been well-received in user testing, with participants praising its intuitive design and smooth user experience. The project demonstrates a strong understanding of user-centered design principles and showcases the ability to translate complex processes into accessible digital solutions.
